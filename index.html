About

Hi! This is Lang Cao, an MSCS student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign now. I am a self-motivated and zealous researcher in Computer & Education.

My research interest and the focus area is knowledge tracing in the automatic education system. I am devoted to developing better systems to help students know their achievements and status during the learning process. I am working on one related project, and I am trying to use deep learning techniques to create a model to monitor student knowledge at each time stage. This model involves the time-aware transformer and text encoder to get information on the description of some questions and studying content. Until now, this model can capture the knowledge of one student from text information in a good performance. In the future, I will adapt this language model to a multi-modal model. It will allow some picture information to be incorporated into the model, which will lead to better outcomes.
